The Owl's School

Καλώς ήρθες! Pass six little quizzes — numbers, colours, days, seasons and classroom words — and earn your Greek diploma.

At the top of a quiet lane stands a tiny schoolhouse with a blue door. Inside, Professor Κουκουβάγια — the wise old owl — is chalking the day’s lesson on the board, ready for a brand-new student. That’s you.

The school has six lessons, and each door stays locked until you pass its little quiz: numbers 1–100, colours, the days of the week, the seasons, and your classroom things. Not perfect grammar. Not flawless spelling. Just the everyday Greek words you’ll actually use.

"Μη ντρέπεσαι. Don't be embarrassed —
a wrong answer is just the lesson saying hello."

Each lesson has the same three steps: listen to the Professor say every word, match the cards, then take a short quiz — and earn a golden key. Collect all six and you graduate, with 64 Greek words tucked safely in your bag. Πάμε; Shall we go?
