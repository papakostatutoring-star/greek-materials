The Owl's Festival

Καλώς ήρθες! Unlock the gates of the great Greek panigíri — one phrase at a time.

High on a sun-warmed hill above the sea, old Σωκράτης the owl is preparing the grandest celebration of the year — το πανηγύρι, the village festival of music, food, and laughter.

But the festival has six golden gates, and each one is sealed. Long ago the wise owl made a rule: only those who carry the living language may pass. Not perfect grammar. Not flawless spelling. Just the warm, everyday words that let a stranger become a friend.

"Μη ντρέπεσαι. Don't be embarrassed —
stumbling over the words is the festival."

Behind each gate, a small puzzle. Listen to the owl speak, match the pictures, choose the meaning — and earn a golden key. Collect all six, and the gates swing open to a feast where a hundred Greek phrases dance around the fire. Πάμε; Shall we go?
